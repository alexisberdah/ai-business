## Market Overview

### Market Size & Trends

## Common Complaints (Market Research)

### Complaint #1: Bloat & Complexity
> "Unnecessary features slow down my project and increase complexity"

**Implication**: Keep v1 **minimal but complete**. No scope creep.

### Complaint #2: Learning Curve
> "Understanding the structure takes time, especially for new developers"

**Implication**: **Prioritize documentation**. Assume zero prior knowledge.

### Complaint #3: Opinionated Structure
> "Enforced structure limits flexibility, integrating third-party libraries is hard"

**Implication**: **Modular architecture**. Easy to replace parts (e.g., swap Stripe for Lemon Squeezy).

### Complaint #4: Abandoned Projects
> "Most free options are abandoned, outdated, or require significant rework"

**Implication**: **Commit to maintenance**. Updates = trust = sales.

**Source**: [Medium Article](https://medium.com/@saasideas/the-pros-and-cons-of-using-next-js-saas-boilerplates-5ec6a0f88252)
